Simple Doro Wat Recipe EMAIL RECIPE 42 Ratings | Rate Now Rating: 3 stars SERVES 4 PREP TIME 30 min TOTAL TIME 1 hr 45 min COOK TIME 1 hr 15 min Ingredients 4 tablespoons clarified butter or ghee 2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger 1 red onion, minced 4 garlic cloves, minced 3 tablespoons berbere spice mixture 1 tablespoon tomato paste 4 cups chicken stock Kosher salt, to taste 4 boneless skinless chicken thighs, halved 1 tablespoon lemon juice 4 hard-boiled eggs Injera or cooked rice, to serve 1 Melt the clarified in a large pot over medium-low heat. 2 Add the ginger, onion, and garlic and sauté until softened, about 20 minutes. 3 Add berbere and tomato paste and continue to sauté until darkened, about 10 minutes. 4 Add chicken stock and season to taste with salt. 5 Add chicken thighs and simmer, covered, until chicken is cooked through, about 45 minutes. 6 Transfer the chicken to a serving plate. 7 Increase the heat to medium-high and reduce the sauce until very thick, about 15 minutes. 8 Season the sauce to taste with lemon juice and salt. 9 Return chicken pieces to the sauce, with hard-boiled eggs. Toss to coat. 10 Serve hot with injera bread or rice. Become a better chef with the MasterClass Annual Membership.
